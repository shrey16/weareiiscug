John Doe | July, 2017

Hello there, Young Padawan!

Welcome to the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. You have much to look forward to in your four years here. And those four years will be something!

What about today, though? Allow us to offer some suggestions for what you can do today, to make your undergraduate program a comfortable one..

1. Do not buy a KROSS cycle from the cycle stall in IISc

This may save you a visit to the health centre. Over the last 2 years, the quality of Kross cycles sold in IISc on the first day has been extremely poor. cycles have broken down while riding.

There are other options for buying a bicycle too. There are multiple established vendors around IISc, and in Bangalore which may be better, safer options.

2. Buy a washing machine

A washing machine in your hostel is very useful. The last 2 UG batches purchased two washing machines each. Each person contributed Rs. 500. Having 2 washing machines for your batch gives enough time for each person to use the machine once a week.

Having a washing machine in the hostel saves the walk from the hostel to the laundry shop every week, and is much cheaper.

3. Set up your savings bank account in SBI, IISc

There are 2 bank branches in IISc, State Bank Of India and Canara Bank. Open an account at SBI today. For your scholarship from KVPY or INSPIRE, a SBI account will be required. Also, having a bank account with the Home Branch nearby helps in easier account related troubleshooting.

4. Set up your WiFi account

WLAN access is an integral part of the UG life at IISc (not to mention, the cheapest way to access internet here). Post registration, each student is provided with a User ID and Password that he can use as login credentials for accessing iiscwlan. iiscwlan is accessible across almost the whole campus (except hostels, and some out of reach places). There are designated wifi rooms in various places - below Main Lecture Hall (MLH), in the Old Physics Building (OPB), and several other departments. LAN ports are also available in the OPB wifi room for faster internet connection speed. It is also worth mentioning that connection across iiscwlan is not secure and some parts of the web are blocked.

Here’s how to get WLAN access:

a. Register online at ‘https://s4.serc.iisc.ernet.in/student/’.
b. Visit SERC room #G10 (subject to change by SERC), between 2PM and 5PM with your ID card to get your account activated.

5. Get acquainted with the campus.

This campus is large and has a lot of places to explore. Take a walk in the evening and get to know where each department is and what the campus has to offer you. If you do manage to get lost, (you won’t be the first one), keep walking, you’ll eventually find your way. Or, you could use the IISc App.

Also, your seniors are very chill people. Meet as many of them as you possibly can. Ask them to take you as a guest to their mess, to try out the food in all the messes and choose your mess accordingly.
