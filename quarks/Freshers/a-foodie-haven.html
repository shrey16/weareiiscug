A Foodie Haven
Bikramjit Karmakar | July, 2017

Once you get a taste of mess food, its time for experiencing options around IISc. If you are a food lover, you shall never be disappointed.

There are primarily two zones:

Zone 1: The Bel Road Zone (Exit from the Bel Road Gate)
This zone hosts mostly premium restaurants ( around Rs 500 for two people)
Zone 2: The MSR Road Zone (Exit from NIAS gate)
The restaurants in this zone are relatively affordable ( Rs 250 for two people) and yet serve brilliant food !

Try and explore restaurants around these two zones. Both of these area are great to go around with friends

Here is a list of restaurants you should definitely try.

1. Truffles Ice and Spice ; Zone 1 ; Rs 800 for two people ; Zomato link

This definitely is one of the best restaurants around IISc Must Try: Sloppy Joe Open Burger, Ferro Rocher Sundae, One Flew over cuckoo's nest , Truffles Rooster Burger.

2. Casino Restaurant; Zone 1 ; Rs 500 for two people ; Zomato link

Must try: Grilled Chicken ; Methi Malai Chicken Curry ;

3. Onesta ; Zone 1 ; Rs 800 for 2 ; Zomato link

Go for Unlimited Pizza and desserts Option, costs around 650 per person incl. of taxes. They also have a promotional ‘Name of the Week’ Offer. Check their Facebook page for more.

4. Chung Wah ; Zone 1; Rs 800 for two people ; Zomato link

Must Try: Hot and spicy lamb, Diced Chilli Chicken

5. Just Shawarma ; Zone 2 ; Starts at Rs 60 ; Zomato link

They make the best shawarmas around IISc

6. Thalaserry ; Zone 2 ; Rs 350 for two people; Zomato link

Brilliant Kerala Cuisine. Must Try: Aappam, Chicken Curry, Fish Curry, Kerala Parota

7. Roy Hotel and Restaurant ; Zone 2 ; Rs 300 for two people ; Zomato link

Bengali Cuisine. Must Try: Doi Katla, Chicken Curry

8. Flavours of North; Zone 2; Rs 300 for two people ; Zomato link

North Indian Cuisine. Must try: Onion paratha, Kadhai Chicken

In case you don't want to go out (which is more likely) you can always get your food delivered.

Foodpanda and Swiggy are two online ordering platform that delivers food from over 150 restaurants to IISc. All the restaurants mentioned above are present on either of the platform Also, you get discounts on your first order.

Best restaurants to order from

1. Little Bites: For Burgers, Wraps etc. Must Try: Breakfast Burrito

2. Absolute Chinese: For great chinese food. Must Try: Chilli Fish

3. Ottupura: For really affordable food. Must Try: Chicken noodles, chilli chicken. ( Absolute Chinese does a much better job in chinese, but Ottupura a way more affordable)
