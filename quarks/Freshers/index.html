Pravega
   Pragyadeep Roy |   July 2017

Pravega is the annual science, tech and cultural festival of IISc Bangalore. Organized mainly by the undergrads, Pravega is pretty young as compared to its fellow college fests of other nationally acclaimed Institutes. In its fifth edition, Pravega is about to make you witness the most awaited fiesta, taking you on a 3-days’ cruise to the carnival of passion away from the long hectic year.
